- DAY 10 -

This was also our first time visiting Yosemite National Park, and what a relief it was to escape the blazing heat of the few days before. I've always personally preferred the greener locations - they can be just as vast and beautiful as any desert. We witnessed an incredible waterfall that flowed 'upward' due to the strong winds. We saw the giant Half Dome which was an incredible spectable. We even saw a few brave mountain climbers scaling the face of the dome!
