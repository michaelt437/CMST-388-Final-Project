- DAY 7 -

This day did not start off on a good note - barely ate any breakfast and instead had snacks because we had to make it to our next hotel in time. Still, the sights we visited have to be my most favorites of the trip. The blue and green of the Colorado River at Horseshoe Bend next to the orange canyon walls made the entire experience surreal. Then, watching the sun set as it seemingly changed the color of the North Rim of the Grand Canyon was so peaceful - nice and quite, unlike the tourist-filled South Rim!
