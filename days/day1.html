- DAY 3 -

The first two days of driving were the toughest - we thought this was a bad idea and wondered how we were actually going to pull this trip off. The morning of the third day, however, our spirits were back up after breakfast, and we started out towards Colorado. The day was mostly spent driving again, but this time there were some cool sights along the way. The most notable one was a giant Van Gogh painting right in the middle of a field. Who knew?!
