About

Welcome to Six Pairs of Shoes!

Last summer, my family and I (6 people!) embarked on a road trip across the country - from Maryland to California! The trip lasted 3 weeks, and each day was a new adventure packed with sights to see and trails to hike.

I created this site to serve as a diary recording our trip day to day. The trip was a huge success, and it was very fun, even if lugging all of our clothes, food, and everyone's shoes (and sandles!) was tough! I'm not much of a photographer, but I tried to take really good pictures with my phone.

I hope you enjoy them, and thanks for visiting!
